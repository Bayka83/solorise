Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

SoloRise is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address to authenticate you and sync your progress across devices.

App data: Your quests, XP, stats, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure cloud (Firebase/Firestore) to enable multi-device access.

Usage data: We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or any data that could be used to track you across apps or websites.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the SoloRise experience — syncing your progress, restoring your account, and enabling app features. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way.

Data Storage

App data is stored on your device (localStorage) and, if you are signed in, in Google Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID. Firebase is compliant with GDPR and other international privacy regulations.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at soloriseapp@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children

SoloRise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
